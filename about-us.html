Our Story

Who are we?

My name is Stoyan and my wife is Vanya. We are small business owners from Varna. We're married with two children.

We share a passion for business especially in the realm of tourism.

How we got into business.

The nineties were pretty tough in Bulgaria. Most of the people, including us, were struggling to make ends meet. I studied in the Naval Academy in Varna and was working as an Officer in one of the bases on the south coast of the country. At that time Military people were making good money and were treated with great respect. There was only one problem - they weren't allowed to speak their minds, especially when it comes to politics. This is something I couldn't cope with, so I quit. That's when the hardship started.

There weren't many opportunities at that time in our country, so the best thing I could do was to join the merchant navy. There was only one caveat - many people wanted to join so It was really hard to get in. I studied for an officer but had to join as a ordinary seaman. I was still happy though - my daughter was just about to be born, so we really needed the money.

It was that first contract with the merchant navy that helped us get off the ground and start a small business - a small appeal store in Varna.

Our First Store.

My wife always has a knack for selling things. She loves everything about it and I can proudly say, that she's the best saleswoman I know.

Our first clothes store was located in one of the markets in the city and was rather small. We didn't like the market much, there was a lot of criminal activity there, but at that time this was the best we could afford. I was still doing contracts on merchant ships so we can save up and open a bigger store.

And so we did - we moved from the market in Varna to Golden Sands - one of the most beautiful resorts on the Bulgarian coast line. This sparked our love in working with tourists.

This move gave me the opportunity to stop sailing and so I did. Both my children were already born, so I wanted to spend time with them. I started working as a Captain on a tug boat in Varna Port - a job I still do and love.

And as for the shop in Golden Sands - it grew from one store to three. And we still operate them today - 20 years later.

Our First Rental.

As much as we like the work on the resort there was one problem with it. It was very tiring and we weren't getting any younger. Working 12 hours a day may be okay when you're thirty, but when you approach 50 it gets harder.

It was time for a change but we first needed a plan. The problem was we didn't have one but luckily we didn't have to devise one either - the perfect plan found us.

We had some money piled up and wanted to buy a house outside the city. We found one in the beautiful village of Strashimirovo - just few kilometers away from Varna and on the coast of Varna lake. The house was in a really poor condition, but the price was good. We bought it and repaired it. And then my wife, being the saleswoman that she is, pitched the idea of renting it out.

To be honest I was really skeptical of that idea. People visit Varna for the buzz of the city, the beaches and restaurants. Who would visit to sleep in a village outside the city. But as you might have already guessed I was wrong.

The very first summer we had got a small group of German workers staying with us for a month. As it turned out the place was close to the industrial area and had people visiting for both pleasure and work. So people kept visiting, and they really liked it.

So there it was - out getaway. Our plan. We I said we didn't device it, it found us.

Nowadays.

Since then we grew our rental portfolio buying 2 small apartments in the center Varna. I'm still working as a Captain on a Tug boat and my wife still runs the stores in Golden Sands. We're still thinking of retirement and not yet ready to do so.

And here you are - this is our story. I hope it gives you a better idea of who we are and helps you build a mental picture of the people you'll stay at when visiting Varna.

Looking forward to meeting you.

This was your story and I hope you liked it. We're looking forward to meeting you!

Description:

You want to visit Varna, but don't want to stay in the busy center? Then why not staying in Strashimirovo Village? This house is one of the very few rentals in the area around Varna lake. It has 5 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a large living-room combined with a kitchen. The 2nd floor balcony opens a beautiful view over the lake and the porch is an excellent place to enjoy some fresh air. The house has 2 gardens - the one in front planted with flowers and grass and the one in the back - a fully fledged garden with many local fruits and veggies. The yard is fenced and suitable for dogs.

The house has all amenities required to make you feel at home. The living-room has a large sofa and big flat-screen tv. The kitchen is fully equipped. It has an oven, microwave, dishwasher, fridge and anything else you may require for a pleasant stay. There is a big barbecue on the porch. There's also a washing machine and a tumble dryer.

This beautiful rental property is located just 15km away from Varna in the small village Strashimirovo. The village is situated on a hill that oversees Varna lake and provides for a beautiful scenery. There are 2 grocery stores in the village where you can find almost anything. The drive to Varna is very easy - it takes around 10 minutes by car via a newly-renovated road. The city can also be reached by train, the train station being a 5 minutes walk away from the house. Parking is abundant and free.

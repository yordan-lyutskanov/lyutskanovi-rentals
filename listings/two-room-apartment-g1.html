Description:

Are you planing to visit Varna? If so, then this 50㎡ apartment might be just for you! It can accommodate 4 adults and is excellent for people who are on a tight budget. It has a small kitchen, a bathroom with a toilet a balcony and 2 separate bedrooms - one equipped with a double bed, the other with 2 single beds.

The place has all the amenities required for a pleasant stay. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, a microwave, stove and all necessary utensils and dishware. There is also free wifi, TV in each bedroom, washing machine, hairdryer and even small barbecue on the balcony.

This apartment comes with a superb location - it is in the central part of the city where you can find anything from restaurants and supermarkets to small appeal stores a drug stores. A 15 minutes walk will take you to Varna's central beach and the biggest park is at the shy distance of several hundred meters. There is a big market in the vicinity where you can buy local fruit and veggies. You should keep in mind that parking in the area is paid and we don't offer any parking places. We can take you to a free parking area just 10 minutes away.
